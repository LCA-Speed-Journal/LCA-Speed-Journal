Team Statistics

Four Categories of Practice Progress: Speed Metrics, X-Factor, Meso-Testing, and Lactic.
Each Practice, we measure one primary metric, depending on the category of the day.
Speed Days, color-coded blue, are all about acceleration and top-speed. The 10m Fly, 30m Block Start, and 40m are used.
X-Factor, color-coded pink, is all about power and athleticism. Six Bounds, Triple Broad Jumps, and Standing Triples are measured.
Meso-Testing, color-coded purple, are metrics we measure at the top of every month. Vertical Jumps and Single-Leg Wall Sits are here.
Lactic Metrics, color-coded orange, are the speed-endurance Days. Broken 300s, The 24/28s Drill, and the 600m 4x4 Predictor are used here.

Progress Scatters

Data per Practice Metric, ranked by gendered percentile. Each metric has its own tab.
Hovering over a data point will give more information. Womens data is in red, Mens in blue.
The Qualifying Bands, seen in the 10m Fly, correspond to speed benchmarks based on the EMAC Conference history.
The upper-bound of the band represents a predicted ~8th place finish, while the lower-bound represents a ~3rd place finish.
