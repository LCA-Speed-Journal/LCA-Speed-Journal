Season Opener: 2023 LCA Speed Trials!

A Recap of the T&F Season Opener: the inaugural Speed Trials had a positive turnout, and some friendly competition offered a great introduction to our season. New technology was used, some discussion about the format of practices looking at the season ahead, and an opportunity to look at where our athletes are beginning their season. Measuring Vertical Jumps, 40m Dashes, and a 10m Fly, see below for the resultant data.

Team Statistics

Leaderboards

A weekly "Practice Leaderboard". Points are assigned to top marks (10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1), and summed by gender, per athlete. Hovering over a bar will give more data to the point totals.

Progress Scatters

Data per Practice Metric, ranked in descending order of performance.

Sum Progress

Conference Standings
